What is the FabLab Mentorship Program?

A 6-month F2F program at the UP College of Fine Arts FabLab. Mentees/interns are trained on fabrication tools and techniques, work on various projects, and get to know the ins and outs of fablab operations.

Who may apply?

Our program is open to enrolled UP Diliman undergraduate students. Priority will be given to CFA students.

How to apply?

Write a letter of intent (.pdf file) addressed to the UP CFA FabLab. Please introduce yourself properly, tell us about your current academic status (course, year level etc), outline key reasons how the mentoring program will impact your field of study.
